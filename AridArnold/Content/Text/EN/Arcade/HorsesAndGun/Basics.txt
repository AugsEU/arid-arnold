<α[140B09]>Controls:
[{Key.ArnoldUp}] - Move Gun Up
[{Key.ArnoldDown}] - Move Gun Down
[{Key.UseItem}] - Fire Gun

Fire your dice-gun at the horses. When a die hits a horse, it will move that many spaces forwards. 

Ex: If a die showing a 3 hits a horse, it will move 3 spaces forwards.

If a horse falls off the left edge of the screen, it's GAME OVER!
